Medan Team 16

We are a data analysis team tasked with analyzing a company's data with the aim of solving existing problems. We are currently assigned to analyze a large company, Superstore.

Superstore

Giant Superstore is a leader in the retail industry with a focus on three main categories: office supplies, technology, and furniture. Superstores, now operating in almost all of the United States.

Superstore experienced some problems, after our data analyst team analyzed the company's profits.

Problems

Superstore has a large profit growth every year, but in some areas it still experiences losses

The profit generated by each state in the US, such as California recorded the highest profit, but on the other hand Texas suffered a loss.

Texas experienced a significant decline in profitability from 2016 to 2017, in contrast to the general trend in some other states.

That's why we created a dashboard to analyze the situation in Texas.
